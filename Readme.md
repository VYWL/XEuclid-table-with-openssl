# XEuclid Table With Openssl

`gcc -o mygcd XEuclid_implementation.cpp -lssl -lcrypto -g`

<br>

`./mygcd <num1> <num2>`

<br>

## Example

<hr>

```{.cpp}
./mygcd 375 275
=-----------------------------------------------------------=
|                       X-Euclid-Table                      |
=-----------------------------------------------------------=
|  q  | r_1 | r_2 |  r  | x_1 | x_2 |  x  | y_1 | y_2 |  y  |
=-----------------------------------------------------------=
|   1 | 375 | 275 | 100 |   1 |   0 |   1 |   0 |   1 |  -1 |  <=  Init
=-----------------------------------------------------------=
|   2 | 275 | 100 |  75 |   0 |   1 |  -2 |   1 |  -1 |   3 |
=-----------------------------------------------------------=
|   1 | 100 |  75 |  25 |   1 |  -2 |   3 |  -1 |   3 |  -4 |
=-----------------------------------------------------------=
|   3 |  75 |  25 |   0 |  -2 |   3 | -11 |   3 |  -4 |  15 |  <=  Finished
=-----------------------------------------------------------=
(a, b) =  75
a =  375
b =  275
x =  -2
y =  3
375 * (-2) + 275 * (3) = 75
```
